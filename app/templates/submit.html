How to get your quote approved:

  • Remove timestamps, laughter, interruptions, parts/joins, and other things which aren't required for the quote to be funny (do not actively improve it).
  • If conveying the humor of the quote requires adding a preface explaining its context, don't bother submitting it.
  • On rare occasions when you need to indicate a time gap between parts of a quote, just use a single blank line.

If you like, you can enter your nick below the quote. This won't be publicly visible and doesn't affect quote approval.
